A game of Dominion in progress

Dominion is a board game by Donald X. Vaccarino in which players build a deck of cards in order to score points during their turns and end the game. I started playing casually in the early 2010s and then more competitively online after starting college, using the username 'tracer' after the alter ego in Calvin and Hobbes. Currently I am a frequent organizer of and participant in online tournaments, and also playtest upcoming expnsions.

More information about the game can be found at Rio Grande Games and the base game is free to play at dominion.games

Accomplishments:

  • 3-time Dominion League Champion
  • Winner of 5 minor tournaments
  • Organized and ran 7 tournaments with up to 67 players
  • Have held the top spot on the online leaderboard
  • Name in the Dominion: Menagerie rulebook
